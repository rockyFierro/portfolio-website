Lets take your idea and turn it into something real that can be communicated clearly and playfully through the internet.

Communicating through play

Learning something new through the act of play is not just for kids. When you need to get your idea out there and make sure it takes hold you need the immediate feedback that only play can provide. Together we can design the best way to tell your story interactively.

The process of illumination

creating illuminated manuscripts isn't just for royalty, the church or fanciful poets anymore. The middle-ages are long gone but the tradition of hiring a scribe to take your idea , your story, your play, your documents and make them even better isn't gone - scribes are developers now - were here to help.

Elements of play in design

trust

For ideas and play to be meaningful you must be able to trust all parties involved.

rules

Everyone who wants to take part can come from any place in life, but we must all agree on how we choose to communicate with each other when we meet.

setting

We make choose the place we communicate with each other first - here where you are looking for listeners and the audience is seeking information.

illumination

The audience is here to read, to listen and learn what story you are telling. In doing so they learn about you - in taking their response and feedback - we learn from them.
